Usage notes

Understand the practical boundary first

Browser PDF utilities are useful for lightweight and temporary jobs. They do not replace professional layout, archival, or enterprise approval systems.

Local file contents

The PDF you choose is read by the current browser. The page may still load site assets, analytics, and advertising requests.

Memory and structure matter

Scanned PDFs, image-heavy files, encrypted documents, or complex internal structures may need more memory and may not process as expected.

Review after processing

Before submitting a result, reopen it and check page count, orientation, readability, and target-platform requirements.
