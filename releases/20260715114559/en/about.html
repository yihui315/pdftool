About us

About PDFTool.work

PDFTool.work is built for everyday office work, study material, application packets, and lightweight document cleanup. The goal is to make common PDF operations clear, understandable, and quick to start.

Principles

Designed for real one-off PDF tasks

Many people only need to shrink a document, combine a few files, remove blank pages, or turn images into a PDF. The site is organized around those high-frequency jobs.

Clarity

Each tool aims to provide a clear entry point, visible status, and reminders to review the result.

Local-first

Tools prioritize browser-side reading of selected PDF contents and result generation, reducing unnecessary server upload flows.

Honest limits

Compression results, processing time, and stability can depend on device memory, browser capability, and PDF structure.

Advertisement

How the site operates

PDFTool.work is based on static pages, frontend scripts, and localized content. We will continue improving multilingual pages and clearer tool guidance.

Current tool scope

The site includes upload preparation, PDF compression, merging, splitting, page management, PDF-to-image conversion, image-to-PDF conversion, rotation, and unlocking.

Ads and analytics

To support maintenance of the free tools, pages may load resources such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. Those requests do not need to read the PDF contents selected inside a tool.

Contact

If a page is unclear, a tool behaves unexpectedly, or you want to discuss cooperation, contact us at contact@pdftool.work.
