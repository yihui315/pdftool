Clear tools with honest boundaries

PDF results depend on the browser, device resources, and the internal structure of the document. The site may also load analytics and ads to support operation.

  • Selected PDF contents are read by the current browser for the tool operation.
  • Very large files can be limited by browser memory, device performance, and PDF structure.

FAQ

About browser-based PDF tools

The tools are designed for the browser to read and process the selected PDF contents locally. The page itself may still request static assets, analytics scripts, or advertising resources.

Use a desktop browser, close memory-heavy tabs, and check whether the PDF is a large scan or has a complex internal structure.

Ad slots help support ongoing maintenance of the free tools. Ads and analytics requests are not used to read the PDF selected in a tool.
