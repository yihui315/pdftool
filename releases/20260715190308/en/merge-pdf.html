Merge PDF FAQ

Are files uploaded to a server?

The tool is designed for the browser to read selected PDF contents and create the result. The page may still load site assets, analytics, or advertising requests.

What should I check after merging?

Before submitting the file, reopen the result and check order, page count, orientation, and target-platform requirements.
